Servicebrok - Client / Agent | Android Apps

 

Servicebrok is an on demand service delivery platform targeted for the Armenian market. Clients instantly post service inquiries through the client application and wait for the agents to apply. Service agent’s log into their own app and instantly view the tasks posted near their location. Users can select one of the applied service agents for their task after viewing each profile, rating and customer’s reviews.
